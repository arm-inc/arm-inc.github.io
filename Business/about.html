About Us

We are 3 undergraduate students, currently studying computer science. Somewhere down the path of enlightenment, we stumbled upon Entrepreneurship and wanted to use our computer skills to enter into this field. ARM's Website Initiative represents our entry into the field with colorful, yet simplistic websites to help people reach out to their target audience. Over the course of the upcoming months we plan to expand beyond Web Development and bring about other services and facilities related to AI/ML and the likes.

We have a strong vision for the world. We actively seek problems and find not just innovative, but fun, recreative and out of the box solutions for them. For these solutions to be marketed and for our dreams to come true, we realised that we needed experience and decided to go ahead with simple, yet fruitful initiatives that would over the course of time help us better understand about the world of entrepreneurship and how to approach our ideas in a way that they stand out in this field. Wish us luck!
